# Final Bilingual Script

## Scene 1

中文：我是灰姑娘。你大概聽過許多關於我的傳聞，但老實說，我並不是故意把玻璃鞋掉在舞會上的。

English: I am Cinderella. You’ve probably heard many rumors about me, but honestly, I didn't mean to lose my slipper at the ball on purpose.

## Scene 2

中文：畢竟，在古老的故事裡，大家都相信晚上十一點五十九分的鐘聲，就是我維持優雅的最後期限。

English: After all, in the old stories, everyone believed that the 11:59 PM chime was the hard deadline for my elegance.

## Scene 3

中文：但你不知道的是，一位聰明的現代女性，從來不需要和時間賽跑。

English: But what you don't know is that a smart modern woman never races against the clock.

## Scene 4

中文：是的，讓優雅永恆延續的秘密魔法，就藏在這個 Uber App 裡。

English: Yes, the secret magic to keeping the elegance everlasting is right inside this Uber app.

## Scene 5

中文：無論何時，它都能為我召來一趟流暢、尊榮的旅程，讓童話裡的南瓜安心留在花園中，成為美麗的裝飾。

English: It summons a seamless, premium journey for me at any moment, leaving the fairy-tale pumpkin as a beautiful ornament in the garden.

## Scene 6

中文：午夜，只是一個數字。願你的每一次出發，都輕鬆而寧靜。

English: Midnight is just a number. May your every departure be effortless and serene.
